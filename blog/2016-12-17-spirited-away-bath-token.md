A handmade replica of the bath token from Spirited Away, carved from wood and finished with paint and gold leaf. Made as a gift for a friend.

![Finished token](/img/posts/spirited-away-token/finished.jpg)

<!--truncate-->

Starting from a couple of offcuts of wood, I cut one down to shape, carved a slot into the top, and sanded it smooth. Painted it red and black to match the tokens from the film, then applied gold leaf for the circle.

![Work in progress](/img/posts/spirited-away-token/process.jpg)

![Holding the finished token](/img/posts/spirited-away-token/held.jpg)

Pretty happy with how it turned out — the gold leaf adds a lot.
