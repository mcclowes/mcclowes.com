---
title: 'Refugee'
authors: mcclowes
tags: [poetry, GloPoWriMo, napowrimo, blackout]
---

Weeping was my lot.  
Half asleep, two little sparrows    
left their own land  
‘refugee’  
I kiss their bodies,  
the colour of wheat in summer.  
They complain into the watering.  
It was my lot to be the cloud!
