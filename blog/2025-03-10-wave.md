---
title: "You Wave"
authors: mcclowes
tags: [writing, poetry]
---
  
You wave  
At everyone.  
Usually goodbye,  
Sometimes hello.  
  
<!--truncate-->    
  
Sometimes they wave back,  
Or smile,  
Or don’t notice you at all.  
  
That’s the worst thing,  
To not be noticed,  
And you shout louder.  
  
I exist  
And you’re leaving my orbit.  
Goodbye.
