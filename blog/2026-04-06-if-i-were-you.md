---
title: 'If I were you…'
authors: mcclowes
tags: [poetry, napowrimo]
---
  
If I were you, mate,  
I’d tell that jumped up boss of yours  
where to stick it.  
  
If I were you, I just wouldn’t take it.  
  
Mate, if I were you,   
I’d dance among the clouds  
and liberate myself of earthly binds.  
Easy.  
  
Honestly, mate,   
I’d walk in that office and tell ‘em  
they’re all full of shit,  
then metamorphose into something winged,  
a flapping fritillary,  
and be done with ‘em.  
  
That’s what I’d do,  
if I were you.
