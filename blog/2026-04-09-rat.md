```
play in car park____________
_____chasing her____________
__shrieks___________________
with ultrasonic laughter____
_________dandelion eyes aglow
_____oblique blurs___________
____________________alert___
__she smells___left_____shape
smells right_________________
______________________blur__
___we dart anywhere make for 
tunnels traces mazes indellible
nothing ______________________
_____her scent______________
______thinning_____________ 
every shadow________________
threat every gap____________________
wrong he retraces___________________ 
retraces_____________ retraces___ 
the route that held her__________
still
then______
there ghost of her___warm__ 
chemical and close__________ 
growing______ louder__________
______________ than sight______
______________________________
shrieks_______________________ 
_________with ultrasonic laughter
```
