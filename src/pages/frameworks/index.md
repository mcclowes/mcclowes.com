# Frameworks

Practical frameworks for product and business thinking. Each includes a shareable one-pager and workshop format.

## The 5Ds of product management

A framework for evaluating PM strengths and hiring for the right fit.

Every PM should have an angle—a specific area of depth: **Design**, **Development**, **Delivery**, **Domain**, or **Data**. Know your gaps before you hire.

## The 4Ps of good business

A framework for building businesses that endure.

**Purpose**, **People**, **Process**, **Product**—each needs to be in place, in order, or you're doomed. Problems at lower levels manifest as symptoms at higher levels.
